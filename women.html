Women Safety & Self-Defense Guide

Jaagrukta hi aapki sabse badi suraksha hai

1. Mahila Suraksha: Ek Samajik Soch

Kyun Zaroori Hai? Aaj ke samaj mein mahilaon ki bhagidari har kshetra mein hai, par unki suraksha abhi bhi ek bada sawal hai. Apradh isliye hote hain kyunki:

  • Mansikta: Samaj mein mahilaon ko kamzor samajhne ki ghalat mansikta.
  • Kanoon ka Darr: Case lamba chalne ki wajah se apradhiyon mein darr ki kami.
  • Jaagrukta ki Kami: Sahi waqt par awaaz na uthana ya dar jana.

2. Self-Defense: Kahan aur Kaise Waar Karein?

Agar koi aap par hamla kare, toh darna nahi hai. In nazuk hisson (vulnerable points) par turant waar karein:

Aankhein (Eyes)

Ungliyon se zor se aankhon par waar karein, isse hamlawar ko dikhna band ho jayega.

Naak (Nose)

Hatholi (palm) se niche se upar ki taraf naak par zor se marein.

Gala (Throat)

Gale ke beech wale hisse par waar karne se hamlawar ki saans ruk sakti hai.
