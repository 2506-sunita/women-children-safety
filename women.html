Ye Kyun Hota Hai?

Mahilaon ke khilaf apradh ka mukhya karan samajik mansikta, kanoon ka darr na hona, aur bachpan se galat parvarish hai. Jab tak hum gender equality nahi layenge, ye sthiti badalna mushkil hai.

Kya Karein (Dos)

  • Hamesha satark (alert) rahein.
  • Phone mein 112 India app rakhein.
  • Zaroorat padne par shor machayein.

Kya Na Karein (Donts)

  • Anjaan rasta na chunein.
  • Anjaan logo se lift na lein.
  • Darr kar chup na rahein.

Situation Mein Kya Karein?

Agar aapko lage ki koi peecha kar raha hai, toh turant kisi bheed wali jagah par jayein. Pepper spray ka istemal karein aur 1091 par call karein.
