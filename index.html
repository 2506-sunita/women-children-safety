1. Women Safety & Self Defense

Aaj ke samay mein har mahila ko apni suraksha ke liye satark rehna chahiye:

  • Awareness: Apne aas-paas ke mahaul par hamesha nazar rakhein.
  • Safe Travel: Sunsaan rasto par akele jane se bachein. Taxi ya Auto ka number ghar walo ko share karein.
  • Digital Tools: Apne phone mein 'Emergency SOS' activate karein aur safety apps (jaise 112 India) install karein.
  • Self Defense: Pepper spray saath rakhein aur basics of self-defense sikhein.

2. Child Safety (Parents Guide)

Bacho ko safe rakhne ke liye unhe ye baatein zaroor sikhayein:

  • Good Touch & Bad Touch: Bacho ko body boundaries ke baare mein pyar se samjhayein.
  • Stranger Danger: Kisi bhi anjaan vyakti se chocolate ya lift na lene ki adat dalein.
  • Open Communication: Bacho se roz baat karein taaki wo bina dare apni baatein aapko bata sakein.

3. Rape Cases: Kaise Bachein aur Kya Karein?

Yadi koi aisi situation ho, toh darna nahi hai, balki kadam uthana hai:

  • Turant shor machayein (Help! Help!).
  • Bheed wali jagah ki taraf bhagein.
  • Yadi accident ho jaye, toh sabse pehle police ko report karein. Medical test se pehle nahayein nahi (evidence ke liye).
