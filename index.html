Ye Sab Hota Kyun Hai?
Samaj mein badhte apradhon ka mukhya kaaran ghalat mansikta aur shiksha ki kami hai. Jab tak hum apne gharon mein ladkon ko mahilaon ka samman karna nahi sikhayenge, tab tak badlav mushkil hai. Kai baar kanoon ka darr na hona bhi apradhiyon ke hausle buland karta hai.

Inhe Kaise Roka Jaye?
1. Self-Awareness: Har mahila ko apne adhikaaron ka pata hona chahiye.
2. Turant Action: Choti se choti badtameezi par bhi turant awaz uthayein.
3. Community Help: Aas-paas ke logon ko ek-dusre ki madad ke liye hamesha taiyar rehna chahiye.
