Good Touch & Bad Touch

Bacho ko samjhayein ki unki body ke "Swimsuit Area" ko koi touch nahi kar sakta. Agar koi aisa kare, toh unhe "NO" bolna hai aur bhag kar parents ko batana hai.

Bacho ko Ye Sikhayein:

  • No Secrets: Koi bhi baat parents se na chupayein.
  • Safe Circle: Sirf Mummy, Papa ya Teacher par bharosa karein.
  • Stranger Danger: Kisi anjaan se chocolate ya khilona na lein.

Emergency: Bachon se related kisi bhi samasya ke liye 1098 par call karein.
