1. Parents ke Liye Tips

Bacho se kaise baat karein? Bacho ke saath rozana dosti wala vyavahar rakhein taaki wo darr ke mare koi baat na chupayein. Unse ye sawal roz puchein:

  • "Aaj school mein sabse achhi cheez kya hui?"
  • "Kya aaj kisi ne aapko aisa touch kiya jo aapko achha nahi laga?"
  • "Kya koi aisi baat hai jo aap mujhe batana chahte ho par darr lag raha hai?"
